Snt of Pharaohs | GAME STRATEGY

Gameplay

SNT of pharos is a PVP and Arena game Depends on Player Experience points. It’s all about the ‘Better Experience points in “PHSNT”. You may not be the best, you just have to be better than your opponent, higher character, weapon and Magical crown level means more Experience points.

Elements

There are 5 different elements present in the game:


Death

Royalty

Martial

Wisdom

Cheating

WEAPONS & CROWNS

There are five tiers of weapons and crowns obtainable in SNT of Pharaohs. The tiers are rated from one star up to Seven stars. The player can mint the weapons and crowns in the market.

CHARACTERS

SNT of Pharaohs has 23 characters. We have Normal, Rare and Super rare characters, The player starts in the game, which is where they can mint their first character. The element of the character is assigned at random.

REWARDS

The player receives two types of rewards when they successfully defeat an opponent, NBW and character experience.

MARKET

The players own their minted NFTs (characters and weapons). The right to sell and trade is wholly owned by the player. SNT of Pharaohs implemented a market. The player can search characters and weapons that have been listed by other players, as well as list their own characters and weapons. There is a small buyer fee added to each transaction.

REQUIREMENTS TO PLAY

SNT Of Pharaohs is a web-based game application. The user will need to have access to the internet to play the game. All gameplay happens on https://phsnt.io contracts are deployed on the Binance Smart Chain.
